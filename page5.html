SDG 12: Responsible Consumption & Production

Ensuring sustainable patterns of consumption and production for a greener future.

What SDG 12 Seeks to Achieve

  • Use natural resources efficiently
  • Reduce pollution & waste
  • Promote sustainable lifestyles
  • Support greener production practices

Key Targets

  • Build national sustainable consumption frameworks
  • Efficient use of natural resources
  • Halve global food waste
  • Environmentally sound chemical & waste management
  • Promote reuse & recycling
  • Encourage sustainability reporting
  • Sustainable government procurement
  • Promote sustainability through education
